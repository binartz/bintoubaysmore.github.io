About

When I was a toddler, I would draw on the walls especially the television screen because I loved the static feel. I even used my bottled milk and hands to decorate the couch by wiping it all over. I always knew I was going to be an artist as that was the main way I expressed myself throughout my life. ...

I use visual and digital art to express myself and learn ways how to positively impact my communities as an African and American woman. I strive to use my creative mind to enforce new ideologies that society forces others to not think about. Hopefuly, I will continue to change the negative narrative on the African diaspora, Africa as a continent, and other minority communities.

While focusing on Studio Art and Computer Science at Brandeis University, I will find ways to expand my network, learn about how black creatives navigate the work industry, and practice and learn more about my interests. You can see how I tackle the creative and tech world through my socials!

About

When I was a toddler, I would draw on the walls especially the television screen because I loved the static feel. I even used my bottled milk and hands to decorate the couch by wiping it all over. I always knew I was going to be an artist as that was the main way I expressed myself throughout my life.

I use visual and digital art to express myself and learn ways how to positively impact my communities as an African and American woman. I strive to use my creative mind to enforce new ideologies that society forces others to not think about. Hopefuly, I will continue to change the negative narrative on the African diaspora, Africa as a continent, and other minority communities.

While focusing on Studio Art and Computer Science at Brandeis University, I will find ways to expand my network, learn about how black creatives navigate the work industry, and practice and learn more about my interests. You can see how I tackle the creative and tech world through my socials!
