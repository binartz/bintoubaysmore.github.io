Bintou Baysmore

Multimedia artist

About

Studio Art and Computer Science major at Brandeis University. I use visual and digital art to express myself and learn ways on how to positively impact my communities as an African American woman. I strive to use my creative mind to enforce new ideologies that society forces others to not think about and hopefully continue to change the negative narrative on African diaspora, Africa as a continent, and other minority communities.
