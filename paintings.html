Self Realization

Oil on canvas, 2023

Finding myself in my craft has been something I was struggling with. I have gotten to the point where I will continue to create what represents me and what I like rather than what I see online. Trying to get into the tech industry has almost steered me away from being creative. I don't have to choose between the two because I can definitely do both. I will continue sharing myself through my work.
